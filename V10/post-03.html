Travelling in Japan

Japan is an island country in East Asia.

With Tokyo as its capital, Japan is situated in the northwest Pacific Ocean, and is bordered on the west by the Sea of Japan, while extending from the Sea of Okhotsk in the north toward the East China Sea, Philippine Sea, and Taiwan in the south.
